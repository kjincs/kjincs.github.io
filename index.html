I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (EECS) at the University of Arkansas. I received my Ph.D. from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC).

Research Interests

Cyber-security, networking, cyber-physical system security and resilience, and modeling and simulation of large-scale systems. More details are available at our Research Group website.

We continually seek strong students to join us for Honors Thesis, Master's Thesis, and Ph.D. Dissertation research, with numerous Research Assistant positions available (click here for details).
