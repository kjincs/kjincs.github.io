Welcome to JinLab

JinLab supports research and education in cyber-security, networking, modeling and simulation of large-scale networks (e.g., Internet, data center, power grid, quantum networks), cyber-physical systems (e.g., smart city, smart grid, IoT). The lab is part of the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (EECS) at the University of Arkansas. JinLab is also involved in computer security and smart grid education at all levels at the University. 


Faculty Member: Dong (Kevin) Jin

Location: University of Arkansas, Fayetteville, AR, 72701
